    • Part 1: Cascade recording system

    In this part, we design to build a cascade recording system, based on the principles of cassette-recorder method, self-targeting CRISPR system, and self-recombination Lambda system.

    Part 2: EL222 directed evolution

    In this part, we design to perform directed evolution on the threshold of EL222 in three directions: improving specificity (reduce leakage), avoiding noises (rise minimum threshold), and increasing sensitivity (lower minimum threshold).

    Part 3: sgRNA screening

    In this part, we design to build a in-silico sgRNA generator and test the efficiency of sgRNAs generated using Batch Random Experimentation Method.

    Part 4: Applications

    In this part, we design to replace EL222 with Pphsa to diagnose IBD, lasR & PluxR system to diagnose Pseudomonas aeruginosa infection, MPhR-pMphR to detect macrolides and Pcau to monitor PCA. We intend to make a proof of concept of applications through promoter replacement and single-level recording.
